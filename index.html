About Our Business

Shopr Scanner provides cutting-edge mobile applications that enable customers to scan product barcodes and complete purchases directly through their smartphones while shopping in retail stores. Our technology seamlessly integrates with existing point-of-sale systems to process legitimate retail transactions, reducing checkout lines and enhancing the overall shopping experience.

Founded in 2025, we are committed to revolutionizing how customers interact with retail environments by providing contactless, efficient, and user-friendly checkout solutions.

Our Services

  • Mobile Barcode Scanning Application - Professional-grade React Native app for iOS and Android
  • Retail POS System Integration - Seamless connection with existing store infrastructure
  • Secure Payment Processing - Encrypted, PCI-compliant transaction handling
  • Digital Receipt Generation - Instant, eco-friendly receipt delivery
  • Real-time Inventory Management - Live stock level integration
  • Analytics Dashboard - Comprehensive reporting for retail partners

Business Model & Pricing

We generate revenue through service fees charged to retail partners when customers successfully complete purchases using our mobile platform. Our pricing structure is designed to be cost-effective for retailers while providing exceptional value through reduced labor costs and enhanced customer satisfaction.

Pricing Model:

  • Transaction-based service fees for retail partners
  • Tiered pricing based on transaction volume
  • Custom enterprise solutions for large retail chains
  • No cost to end-users (customers)

Technology & Security

Our platform is built using enterprise-grade technology stack including React Native for mobile applications, Node.js backend infrastructure, PostgreSQL databases, and Stripe payment processing. All transactions are processed securely through certified payment processors with end-to-end encryption.

Security Features:

  • PCI DSS compliant payment processing
  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure authentication and authorization
  • Regular security audits and compliance monitoring

Target Market

  • Grocery and convenience stores
  • Electronics and specialty retail
  • Multi-location retail chains
