The Future of Retail Payments

Scan-and-Go Checkout. Instant Settlement. Zero Chargebacks.

SwiftShopr revolutionizes how retailers process payments and how customers checkout. Powered by blockchain-based stablecoin rails and instant mobile payments.

Two Powerful Services

🛒 Scan-and-Go Checkout

Customers scan product barcodes as they shop, building their cart in real-time. At the exit, they pay directly through the SwiftShopr app—no lines, no cashiers needed.

  • Real-time cart building
  • Direct mobile payment
  • Instant receipt
  • Zero checkout lines
  • Customer cashback rewards

⚡ QR Code Payment

Traditional checkout experience enhanced. Customers scan a QR code on your payment terminal and confirm payment instantly through their phone—faster than cards.

  • Fast mobile checkout
  • Works on existing terminals
  • No hardware changes
  • Instant settlement
  • Customer cashback rewards
